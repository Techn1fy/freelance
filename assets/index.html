About Me

Skincare Is Not Just a Passion — It's Her Expertise

Sungjemlila Longkumer is a certified cosmetologist from India with a deep, science-backed love for skincare culture. Growing up fascinated by ingredients, rituals, and the transformative power of a well-built routine, she turned her expertise into a platform where authenticity is the only currency.

She creates honest, research-led product reviews for cosmetic and skincare brands — reaching a highly engaged community of over 528K beauty enthusiasts who come back because they trust her. No filters on the opinion, only on the photo.

Recognised as NDTV India's 2023 Skincare Influencer of the Year, her work bridges the gap between beauty science and real-life application — in a space too often flooded with paid promotions, she leads with credibility.

For Sungjemlila, beauty is never a trend. It's a ritual — personal, evolving, and deeply worth caring about.
